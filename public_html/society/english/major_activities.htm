Major Activities

(1) Annual meetings , Symposia, Seminars, etc.
Å@The Annual Meetings twice a year, in spring and in fall, are held for three days. During the meetings, about 180 technical papers are presented. At the meetings, the latest studies, findings, and information are exchanged through technical sessions, lectures and exhibitions. Planning committee, Research committee, International Cooperation committee, and Local chapters offer more than 20 symposia, seminars or international workshop at suitable occasions.

(2) Activities ( Role) of Regular Committees
Å@JILM has nine regular committees and some branch or ad-hoc committees to accomplish major tasks in the objectives of JILM. The roles of regular committees are briefly shown in the diagram of the JLM Organization.
Research Committee, one of the regular committees, has 11 branch committees listed below. All of the committees consist of researchers both from industry and academic, and they present the accumulated results by publishing research reports or by holding symposia as occasion demands.

(i) High Pressure Casting/ Solidification of Aluminum Alloys
(ii) Painted Surface Technology
(iii) Dynamic Deformation and Strength of Aluminum Alloys
(iv) Mechanism for the Formation of Ground Powder in Aluminum Cold Rolling
(v) Physical Metallurgy Related to Grain Boundaries
(vi) Rolling Production Technology in Aluminum
(vii) Aluminum Structural Adhesion and Joining
(viii) Reaction between Molten Aluminum and Refractory Materials
(ix) Mechanism for the Surface Defect Formation in Aluminum Extruded Shapes
(x) Development of Rapidly Solidified P/M Aluminum Alloys
(xi) Friction Utilizing Solid Phase Joining
